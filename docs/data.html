Available data

Healthcare

Infected
Frequency: Daily
Level: Municipality/District
Source: MSIS
Preview:
Github:
Download: csv xlsx

Case data is extracted daily at 4 AM from the Norwegian Surveillance System for Communicable Diseases (MSIS) web interface. Municipalities and districts in MSIS are automatically mapped to current municipalities (kommuner) and districts (bydeler) based on Statistics Norway`s (SSB) classifications. Note that district cases for Oslo and Bergen often sum to a slightly lower number of cases than the respective municipality cases. To report cases at the most granular level, the difference is mapped to "Ukjent bydel"("Unknown district") for Bergen and Oslo.

Economics

Business Compensation Scheme
Frequency: Daily
Level: Counties
Source: Tax Administration
Preview:
Github:
Download: csv xlsx

The Business Compensation Scheme provides financial compensation for enterprises with large drops in revenue resulting from the virus outbreak. Information about the enterprises and the amounts they receive will be made publically available shortly after their applications have been approved. Information from the Compensation Scheme is updated daily.
